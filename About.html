THE STORY

How did we get trapped find each other?

The Mechanisms were created by Dr Carmilla (Maki Yamazaki) to form her backing band in late 2010. When the doctor decided to concentrate on her solo work, they threw her out of an airlock and struck out on their own- The new captain (first mate) being Jonny D’Ville (Jonathan Sims) They work mainly from their homeport of Oxford, but since 2012 they’ve also presented several well-received shows at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival. They disembanded in 2020.
